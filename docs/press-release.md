**PranaPulse Unveils the “Vitality Social Network”: Turning Daily Selfies into Proactive Wellness Actions**

**SEATTLE, WA** — PranaPulse today announced the launch of its groundbreaking wellness platform, a first-of-its-kind “Vitality Social Network” that transforms standard smartphone cameras into sophisticated health sensors. By combining advanced remote photoplethysmography (rPPG) with a proactive “Nudge Engine,” PranaPulse moves beyond traditional health logging to provide users with real-time, actionable insights based on their body’s unique rhythms.

While most health gadgets act as passive digital diaries—collecting mountains of data that users often find difficult to interpret—PranaPulse is designed as a proactive companion. It focuses on the “Daily Glow,” a 20-second facial scan that measures Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and recovery, translating complex biomarkers into a simple, social-first experience.

“We didn't want to build another clinical tool that makes users feel like patients,” said Satish Jonnala, Founder of PranaPulse. “PranaPulse is built on the idea that wellness should be warm, welcoming, and shared. We’ve replaced the ‘silly logger’ approach with an action engine that doesn't just tell you your heart rate is high—it tells you to take two minutes to breathe because it notices you’re running hot before a big meeting.”

**The Hybrid Advantage: Daily Glow vs. Weekly Deep Dive**
PranaPulse introduces a dual-fidelity approach to personal health. The **Daily Glow** uses rPPG technology to extract vitals through a simple selfie video, allowing users to maintain “Vitality Streaks” with their friends and family. For those seeking deeper insights, the **Weekly Deep Dive** utilizes contact-based PPG—where users place a thumb over the camera lens—to unlock advanced metrics like arterial stiffness and vascular age.

**A Proactive Action Engine**
At the core of the app is the Nudge Architecture. Instead of static charts, users receive dynamic recommendations. If the system detects a significant drop in a user’s HRV baseline, it might suggest an earlier wind-down routine or a guided breathing session. Conversely, when a user is in a high-vitality state, the app encourages them to tackle their most challenging physical or mental tasks for the day.

**Strava for Wellness: The Vitality Circle**
Borrowing from the best of social fitness, PranaPulse features “Vitality Circles.” Users can join private groups where they share their “Glow” rather than raw medical data. This creates a supportive environment where community members can send “Breath Kudos” to friends who are stressed or celebrate a teammate’s 30-day consistency streak.

**Privacy-First by Design**
PranaPulse is built with a “Privacy-First” philosophy. All rPPG and acoustic analysis are performed directly on the user's device. No raw video or voice recordings are ever stored in the cloud; only the derived wellness trends are used to power the social and insight engines.

**Availability**
PranaPulse is available starting today for early access users in the Seattle area, with a global rollout planned for later this year. The app is compatible with most modern iOS and Android devices, requiring no additional hardware or wearables.

**About PranaPulse**
Based in Seattle, PranaPulse is a wellness technology company dedicated to making health insights accessible, actionable, and social. By leveraging cutting-edge computer vision and signal processing, PranaPulse helps individuals live in sync with their bodies.
